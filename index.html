Work With Dignity

Cumberland River Services was founded on one belief: every person deserves the chance to work with dignity and respect. We actively connect returning workers, individuals overcoming homelessness, and those with complicated backgrounds to employers who will give them a real shot.

We partner with Nashville nonprofits, shelters, and reentry programs to build a bridge between people who need work and employers who need workers. We vet, we support, and we stay in the loop.

Returning CitizensWe connect qualified workers with felony histories to employers who understand second chances.
Homeless Workforce Re-entryWe partner with Nashville shelters and services to help people returning to work find stable employment.
Employer MatchingWe match workers to employers based on skills, location, and work history. No guesswork.
Ongoing SupportWe stay in contact with both sides. Feedback flows both ways. Problems get solved early.
